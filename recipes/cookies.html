Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These delicious chocolate chip cookies are big, fat, and delicious! With only a total time of 40 minutes, these cookies are easy enough to bake, and this recipe will give you 18 servings.

Mix flour, baking soda and salt together, and set it aside. Cream your melted butter with brown and white sugar in a bowl, until well blended. Then add vanilla and egg until light and creamy. Add your chocolate chips as a final touch. Put decent sized chunks of dough on a greased cookie sheet, and put them in a preheated oven at 165 degrees C, for about 15 to 17 minutes.
